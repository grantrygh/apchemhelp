About

Who am I?

Well, to start, my name is Grant Rygh and I am currently a freshman attending the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I'm not a professional web developer, nor am I a chemist, however I do possess some knowledge in chemistry, as well as some coding skills that I began learning in the summer of 2013.

Why did I build APChemHelp?

I created this website for one purpose: to serve as a resource for other students taking a chemistry class, whether it's at the regular, AP, or collegiate level.

Something is broken!

If you find any errors on the website, please shoot me an email through the the contact page. I will be updating the site regularly to sort out bugs and add new content.

Can I help contribute?

If you are a teacher or student and would like to help out or donate content/study materials, please contact me. We are also looking for help to build new features. The application is open-sourced and can be found on GitHub. Come help build APChemHelp with us!
